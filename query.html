On load the PubMed ID search is disabled, click on the check box to enable it. The default PubMed ID, 20093466, is The genetic landscape of a cell by Costanzo M., et al.

Publications ar listed as: PMID Col14 Interactions:### where PMID is the PubMed ID, Col14 is column 14 from the TAB2 file: First author surname of the publication in which the interaction has been shown, optionally followed by additional indicators, e.g. Stephenson A (2005), and ### is the number of interactions. Results with over 15000 edges will only display low throughput edges by default.
